Krylov Quantum Diagonalization

Krylov Quantum Diagonalization is a quantum algorithm for diagonalizing large many-body Hamiltonians. It is based on the Krylov subspace method, which is a numerical method for finding the eigenvalues and eigenvectors of a matrix. The algorithm is implemented in the Krylov-Quantum-Diagonalization repository.
