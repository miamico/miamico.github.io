I am doing my Ph.D in Physics in circuit quantum electrodynamics (cQED), especially focusing on quantum vacuum phenomena which can be observed in superconducting qubits systems as the dynamical Lamb effect and the dynamical Lamb effect. I got my Bachelor's degree in Physics Engineering from Politecnico di Milano and Master's degree in Physics from University of Milan in Italy. In Aug. 2016 I came to United States to start my Ph.D in New York City. My advisor is Prof. Roman Kezerashvili.
