Quantum Algorithms & Applications

I design algorithms that run on today’s noisy hardware, including Krylov sub-space diagonalization of many-body Hamiltonians (56-qubit demos, 2024) and early experimental realizations of Shor’s factoring algorithm.

Quantum Control & Error Mitigation

My team pioneered deep-reinforcement-learning pulses that halve two-qubit error rates, and context-aware compilers that mitigate correlated noise by 18 % layer-fidelity gains.

Benchmarking Standards

I co-authored the IEEE-QCE 2023 paper Defining Standard Strategies for Quantum Benchmarks, outlining fair, hardware-agnostic metrics for NISQ devices.
